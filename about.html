About

I'm originally from Denver, and I currently study Computer Science at University of Michigan's College of Engineering. I originally got into CS as a kid learning how to edit animations on MicroWorlds. Growing up, my joy in being creative only grew, so my passions include crafting and art. So when I'm not coding, I'm often sculpting clay, drawing on my iPad, or making jewelry!
